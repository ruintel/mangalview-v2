Deluxe Rooms

Our deluxe rooms offer a luxurious experience that will make your stay truly unforgettable. These spacious rooms are elegantly furnished and equipped with all the amenities you need for a comfortable and relaxing stay. With large windows offering plenty of natural light and stunning views, our deluxe rooms are the perfect choice for guests seeking the ultimate in comfort and luxury.

Banquet Hall

Our hotel's spacious banquet hall is the perfect venue for all your conference and meeting needs. Whether you're hosting a business presentation, a training session, or a conference, our banquet hall is well-suited for all types of events. With ample seating for up to 50 guests, our banquet hall offers a comfortable and elegant setting for your event.

Equipped with state-of-the-art audiovisual technology, including projectors and sound systems, our banquet hall makes it easy to deliver impactful presentations and meetings. Our professional and experienced event staff will work with you to ensure your event runs smoothly, from setting up the space to providing catering services.

Our banquet hall also offers the flexibility to accommodate different seating arrangements, including theatre-style, classroom-style, and boardroom-style. Whether you're looking for an intimate setting for a small meeting or a large space for a conference, our banquet hall is the perfect choice.

With ample light and modern decor, our banquet hall offers a welcoming and professional atmosphere that will impress your guests. Contact us today to book our banquet hall for your next conference or meeting.
